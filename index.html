About Fairfield FFA

Located in the agricultural town of Fairfield Montana, we are truly living to serve. MT0025 represents more than just a code we write in the top of our CDE Scan-tron, it represents achievements won by past and present generations. It represents the struggles and hardships of former alumni. It represents the promise of better days through better ways.


Our chapter firmly believes that American agriculture can and will hold true to the best traditions of our national life and that our chapter can and will exert an influence in our homes and community, which will stand solid for our part in that inspiring task.
